# Relationship graph

The **relationship graph** shows your book's whole web of relationships
as an interactive map: one node per Story Bible entry, one coloured line
per relationship. It complements the [Arc View](arc-view.md) (timeline)
and the [Storyboard](../books/storyboard.md) (pacing) as the third
visual planning view.

## Opening

In the chapter sidebar, click **Relationship graph** (only shown when
the Story Bible is active). The URL switches to `?view=relationships`.

## Nodes and lines

- **Nodes** are shaped and coloured by type (character, setting, item,
  plot point, lore) and carry the name + an icon.
- **Lines** are coloured by relationship type: ally (green), rival
  (red), family (blue), mentor (purple), romantic (pink), neutral
  (grey) - the same legend as the Arc View.
- Nodes are freely draggable; the view lays them out in a circle to
  start.

## Creating relationships visually

Drag from the edge of one node onto another node. A small dialog opens:
pick the relationship type and optionally add a note. **Add** stores the
relationship on the source entry and it appears immediately as a new
line.

## Deleting relationships

Click a line and confirm the prompt - the relationship is removed.

## Jumping to an entry

- A **single click** on a node opens a detail panel (name, type,
  relationship count) with **Open in editor** and **Show appearances**.
- A **double click** opens the entry directly in the Story Bible
  editor.
