# Collapsible sidebars

The editor has collapsible sidebars so you can hand the full width to your text
— especially useful on a phone, a tablet, or a narrow window.

- **Left sidebar** — the chapter list (prose books) or the page thumbnails
  (picture-books and comics).
- **Right sidebar** — the properties pane (picture-book and comic editors),
  shown next to the canvas.

## Toggling a sidebar

Each sidebar has a **collapse button** at its edge (a chevron, labelled
"Collapse sidebar" / "Seitenleiste einklappen"). Click it to slide the sidebar
shut; click again to bring it back. The chapter / page list and the canvas
expand to fill the freed space.

Your choice is remembered **per browser, per device** (it is stored locally),
so the editor reopens the way you left it.

## On small screens

The sidebars are **viewport-aware**:

- **At or above 1200px** wide (a desktop window), the sidebars start
  **expanded**.
- **Below 1200px** (phones, tablets, narrow windows), they start
  **collapsed** — so the first thing you see on a phone is the writing area,
  not the chapter list. Tap a collapse button to open a sidebar when you need
  it.
- **Below 768px**, the two sidebars are **mutually exclusive**: opening one
  automatically closes the other, so a sidebar never covers the canvas on a
  small screen.

If you open the editor on your phone and "the chapters are gone", they are not
gone — the left sidebar is just collapsed. Tap its toggle to open it.
