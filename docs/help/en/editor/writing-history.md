# Writing history

The **writing history** shows how much you have written over time. It
is the detailed counterpart to the [writing-goal widget](writing-goals.md)
on the dashboard: instead of just today, you see a whole period with
statistics, a per-day chart, and a breakdown by book and chapter.

## Opening

In the writing-goal widget on the dashboard, click the **History**
button.

## Time window

At the top, pick the window: **Last 30 / 90 / 365 days**. All
statistics and charts update accordingly.

## Statistics

- **Total words** in the window.
- **Active days** (days with net positive words written).
- **Avg per active day**.
- **Current streak** and **longest streak** of consecutive active
  days.

Below them, a bar chart shows the words written per day.

## By book and chapter

The **By book** list shows words per book in the window. Clicking a
book expands the per-chapter breakdown. Words whose chapter has since
been deleted collect under **Deleted chapters** and stay attributed to
the book.

## CSV export

**Export CSV** downloads the daily history for the selected window as a
CSV file (`day,words_written`) - handy for your own analysis or
spreadsheets.

## How the data is collected

Whenever you save a chapter, Bibliogon counts the change in word count
and credits it to that book + chapter for the current day. Only net
words written are counted; the daily goal itself is per-device and does
not feed the history.
